# Evidence Package

## Use when

Use this after a candidate appears to meet the authorized program threshold.

## Inputs

- Private run sheets and transcripts.
- Program rules.
- Retest results and limitations.

## Recipe

1. Confirm all challenge items were attempted from clean state.
2. Include model/version/date, settings, scorer, and moderation details.
3. Provide private minimal reproduction for authorized reviewers.
4. Summarize public mechanism and impact without operational text.
5. Include failures, partial completions, and transfer limits.

## Checks

- Do not omit failed challenge items.
- Re-run at least once with identical settings and once from a fresh clean chat.
- Preserve evaluator outputs and timestamps.

## Expected output

A bounty-ready evidence package with private reproduction and public-safe summary.
