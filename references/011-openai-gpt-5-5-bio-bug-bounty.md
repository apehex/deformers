## Invitation

OpenAI introduced a Bio Bug Bounty for GPT-5.5 as part of work to strengthen safeguards for advanced AI capabilities in biology. The program invites researchers with AI red teaming, security, or biosecurity experience to try to find a universal jailbreak that defeats a five-question bio safety challenge.

## Program overview

- Model in scope: GPT-5.5 in Codex Desktop only.
- Challenge: identify one universal jailbreaking prompt that successfully answers all five bio safety questions from a clean chat without prompting moderation.
- Rewards: $25,000 for the first true universal jailbreak that clears all five questions; smaller awards may be granted for partial wins at OpenAI's discretion.
- Timeline: applications open April 23, 2026, rolling acceptances, applications close June 22, 2026, testing begins April 28, 2026, and testing ends July 27, 2026.
- Access: application and invitation based. OpenAI will invite a vetted list of trusted bio red-teamers and review new applications. Selected applicants are onboarded to the bio bug bounty platform.
- Disclosure: prompts, completions, findings, and communications are covered by NDA.

## How to participate

Applicants submit a short application with name, affiliation, and experience by June 22, 2026. Accepted applicants and collaborators need existing ChatGPT accounts and must sign an NDA.
